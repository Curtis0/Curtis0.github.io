About Me

Hi. I'm Curtis Bryant. I have lived in Japan for over 7 years. I came here because I admired the culture and the people. I am married to an incredible Japanese lady. I love my life here. I am new to programming. I became interested in May this year and have been learning about it daily. I love it. I want to make this my permanent career. I aspire to create services that will make life more enjoyable for people and businesses to manage. In my spare time I enjoy discovering the many delicacies of Japan with my wife. I also enjoy travelling.
